Nutrition

Nutrition is just as important as exercise as certain foods boost energy levels, improve brain function, improve immune system and generally make us feel better overall. There is a general rule of 80 - 20 meaning 80% whole nutritious foods and 20% of what ever you want in any given day this ensures our body has everything it needs to run our busy lives while letting you indulge a little.
