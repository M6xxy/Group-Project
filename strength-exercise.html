Strength Exercises Explained

There are many types of strength exercises ranging from bodyweight, free weights, machines or even yoga to name a few, the best approach would be to find what you wouldn’t mind doing regularly and go from there. Now strength exercises aren’t just about lifting weights they can be something as simple as hill walking or hiking as these activities will boost leg strength over time, or things like gardening if it requires a lot of digging or shoveling can boost upper body strength as well.
